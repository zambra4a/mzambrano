How did I get where I am now?


I was born in Seville, a city located in the south of Spain, in Andalusia. When I was a child, I was constantly moving to different cities because of my parents job. I got to know a lot of people and discovered a lot of different places. Then, when I was old enough to start reading books, I became a reading addict. I spent hours reading every day, and I loved it so much that I decided I wanted to be a literature teacher. And that was the idea, until I got my first computer when I was 13. From that moment on, everything changed. I discovered a whole new world of possibilities. At the begining, I broke my PC hundreds of times, and everytime I broke it, I sent it to a local PC shop to get it fixed.

Then, I learnt to fix it by myself, and it was wonderful. No more money spent on that. After that, I began playing games on my computer and, soon after that, I realized I did not want to just play a game, I wanted to create items for the game, I wanted to make my own enemies. So, I did it. I learnt how to modify the game files so I was able to add things to the game. That was insane for me. It seemed like magic. Then, I continued learning things about programming and I decided I no longer wanted to be a teacher, I wanted to be a programmer, a virtual magician, someone who creates things from nothing. Since then, I have never stopped learning, experimenting and improving my skills. Now, I am a developer, and I love doing what I do.
